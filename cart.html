Frequently asked

Do you take custom orders?
Yes — send us the design or photo on WhatsApp and we will quote you.

How long does delivery take?
Inside Dhaka: 2–3 days. Outside Dhaka: 4–7 days via courier.

What payment methods do you accept?
Cash on delivery, bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and bank transfer.

Yes — we send photos and videos before shipping, and you can visit our workshop in Dhaka by appointment.
