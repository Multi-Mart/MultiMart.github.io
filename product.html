The Piece

Classical Bust No. 8 takes its form from the European neoclassical tradition — the same quiet geometry you see in marble galleries across Europe, made accessible for the modern Bangladeshi home. It works in a reading nook, on a console table, or beside a stack of books.

Craftsmanship

Each piece is machine-cut for precision, then hand-finished in our Dhaka workshop — sanded, painted, and inspected before it ships. We use a resin composite base with a marble-tone finish: lighter than real marble (1.2 kg, easy to move), but with the visual weight and texture that makes a classical bust feel timeless.

Care

Dust with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid direct sunlight for long periods (it can soften the finish over years). Don't use water or chemical cleaners. If the piece ever loses its lustre, send us a photo on WhatsApp — we'll refinish it for you at cost.
